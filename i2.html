History of Pasta

Pasta has a rich history dating back to ancient Italy, with evidence of pasta-like dishes consumed by the Etruscans and Romans. The word "pasta" is Italian for "dough" or "pastry".

Types of Pasta

1. Spaghetti: Long, thin, cylindrical strands

2. Fettuccine: Wide, flat noodles made from egg and flour

3. Penne: Tube-shaped pasta, great for chunky sauces

4. Linguine: Long, flat, and narrow noodles
