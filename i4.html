What is Spaghetti?

Spaghetti is a type of Italian pasta made from wheat flour and water. It's a long, thin, cylindrical strand of pasta that's a staple in Italian cuisine.

Fun Facts

1. Spaghetti is one of the most popular pasta shapes worldwide.

2. The world's longest spaghetti strand measured over 3,700 feet!

3. Spaghetti is a staple in Italian cuisine, often served with a variety of sauces.
