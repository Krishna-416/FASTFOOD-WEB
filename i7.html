History of Coffee

Coffee originated in Ethiopia and spread throughout
the Arabian Peninsula in the 15th century.Today, coffee is enjoyed worldwide.

Benefits of Coffee

1. Cognitive Function: Coffee can improve alertness, focus, and mental performance.

2. Antioxidants: Coffee contains antioxidants, which can help protect against cell damage.

3. Mood Boost: Caffeine in coffee can help improve mood and reduce symptoms of depression.
