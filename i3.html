Sandwich Fun Facts

1. The sandwich is named after John Montagu, the 4th Earl of Sandwich,
who lived in the 18th century.

2. The world's largest sandwich weighed over 5,000 pounds.

Types of Sandwiches

1. Club Sandwich: A triple-decker sandwich with turkey, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise.

2. Grilled Cheese: A classic sandwich made with melted cheese between two slices of bread.

3. BLT: A simple yet flavorful sandwich with bacon, lettuce, and tomato.
