Benefits of Lemon Tea

1. Boosts Immune System: Lemon tea contains vitamin C, which can help support immune function.

2. Aids Digestion: Lemon tea may help stimulate digestion and relieve symptoms of indigestion.

Types of Lemon Tea
1. Black Tea with Lemon: A classic combination of black tea and lemon slices.

2. Green Tea with Lemon: A refreshing and light tea option.

3. Herbal Lemon Tea: A caffeine-free option made with herbs like lemon balm or lemon verbena.
